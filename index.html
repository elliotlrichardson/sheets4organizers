Google Sheets for Organizers

Created by Elliot L. Richardson · Email me!

Hey everyone! My name is Elliot, it’s a pleasure to meet you :^)

I work in progressive data and have a frickin’ blast noodling around in Google Sheets. There are a lot of tips and tricks that have been helpful to me as a data director for a youth organizing team (hi NextGen Maine) and as a data manager at the Sunrise Movement. But I know not every team has a data director; my coworkers and I on the MA for Warren team did not have a data director or any data staff and we did our best until the best full-time volunteer of all-time joined the squad (s/o to Tanay, you are truly the best). That experience as an organizer helped me understand what is useful to know and my experiences in data have taught me how to build tools to deliver that information to the absolute heroes doing the actual organizing. But if you don’t have someone delivering that information to you, I’m hoping I can help you deliver it to yourselves and keep doing the amazing organizing that you are doing!

So with that goal in mind, I have done my best to create and compile explanations and tutorials in a coherent way for y’all. Right off the bat, I’d like to say that I do not have teaching experience and am very open to feedback. (!!!!!!!) I hope that the following information is helpful to you and I’d love to chat with you about it or answer questions as best as I can. My email is elliotlrichardson@gmail.com and I will be twiddling my thumbs just waiting for you to use me as a resource in the pursuit of collective liberation on a thriving planet.
